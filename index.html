About Nova

Nova Outreach was founded on the belief that every generation has something valuable to offer, and that the connections between young and old can transform communities. We are a student-led non-profit organization dedicated to wellness, care, and preserving the wisdom of our elders.

✦

Our Mission

To bridge generational divides through meaningful programs that promote mental and emotional wellbeing, preserve life stories and memories, and create lasting connections between students and seniors in our community.

Our Vision

A world where every senior's story is valued and preserved, where intergenerational connections flourish, and where both young and old thrive through shared experiences and mutual respect.

Our Values

  • Compassion and empathy in all our interactions
  • Respect for the wisdom and experiences of our seniors
  • Commitment to preserving stories and memories
  • Dedication to promoting mental and emotional wellbeing
  • Excellence in service and community impact
✦

Why Nova?

The name "Nova" represents a new beginning, a bright light, and a powerful force for positive change. Just as a nova illuminates the cosmos, we aim to shine light on the invaluable stories and experiences of our senior community while bringing joy and purpose to all involved.

Games Team

The Games Team brings joy, laughter, and cognitive stimulation to retirement homes through carefully designed activities and interactive programs. We believe that play is ageless and that games can build bridges between generations.

✦

What We Do

  • Organize weekly game sessions at partner retirement homes
  • Develop age-appropriate and accessible game programs
  • Facilitate classic games like bingo, trivia, and card games
  • Create innovative intergenerational activities
  • Adapt games to accommodate various physical and cognitive abilities
  • Host special game tournaments and themed event days

Our Impact

Game sessions provide more than entertainment—they offer cognitive stimulation, social interaction, and emotional connection. Many seniors report reduced feelings of loneliness and increased mental sharpness. Students gain patience, empathy, and an appreciation for the wisdom and humor of older generations.

Skills We Value

Event planning, interpersonal communication, creativity in activity design, patience, enthusiasm, and an understanding of senior care and accessibility needs.

Musical Team

The Musical Team harnesses the universal language of music to bring joy, comfort, and connection to retirement homes. Through live performances and interactive music sessions, we create moments of beauty and emotional resonance that transcend age.

What We Do

  • Perform live music at retirement homes and community events
  • Lead sing-along sessions featuring classic songs seniors love
  • Organize small ensemble performances (piano, guitar, vocals, etc.)

Senior Story Project

The Senior Story Project is the heart of Nova Outreach—a transformative initiative that connects students with seniors to record and preserve life stories, memories, and wisdom. Through thoughtful conversations, we create a digital legacy that honors our elders and enriches future generations.

✦

Our Mission

To preserve the invaluable life experiences, wisdom, and memories of seniors in retirement homes while promoting mental and emotional wellbeing through meaningful intergenerational conversations.

How It Works

  • Students are paired with senior participants at partner retirement homes
  • Regular conversation sessions are scheduled over several weeks
  • Trained students conduct thoughtful interviews using story prompts
  • Sessions are recorded (with permission) and carefully transcribed
  • Stories are edited and compiled into beautifully designed digital books
  • Each senior receives a personal copy of their story
  • Stories are added to our growing digital library series

The Impact

For Seniors: The project provides validation, purpose, and social connection. Sharing life stories has been shown to reduce feelings of loneliness, improve mood, and enhance overall wellbeing. Many participants report feeling heard and valued in ways they haven't experienced in years.

For Students: Participants gain perspective, empathy, and wisdom that can only come from lived experience. Students develop better listening skills, historical understanding, and appreciation for different generations. Many form lasting bonds with their senior partners.

For Families: The digital books become treasured family heirlooms, preserving stories and memories that might otherwise be lost. Families gain insight into their loved ones' experiences and feel gratitude for the care and attention given.

The Digital Book Series

Each collected story becomes part of our growing digital library—a series of books that celebrate the rich tapestry of human experience. These publications serve multiple purposes: they honor seniors, educate younger generations, preserve historical perspectives, and demonstrate the value of intergenerational connection.
